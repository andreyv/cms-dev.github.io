We are aware of the following projects related to CMS. These projects are not developed nor maintained by us, hence we do not give guarantees or support for them.

  • CCreater, a graphical application for Linux or Windows developed by Kiên Nguyễn Tiến Trung, that make it easier to transform a contest in the Italian format, and hence to import it in CMS. Sources are here.
  • A fork, by William Di Luigi and Luca Versari, adding some social features to CMS (for example, a forum), and used by the Italian Olympiads.
  • A fork by Masaki Hara, which has support for external login providers (for example Facebook and Twitter), and is used by the Japanese Olympiad.
